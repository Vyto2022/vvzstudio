Lead routing flow.

1) The visitor submits a form.

The landing page captures the contact request with the key fields.

2) The API stores the lead.

The backend validates and saves the lead data for operations and future reporting.

3) Telegram gets the alert.

The team receives the lead card instantly and starts the follow-up process.
