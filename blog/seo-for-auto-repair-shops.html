SEO for auto repair shops.

Local SEO for a garage starts with the structure of the site, not with endless blogging. Home, service pages, city relevance, schema, reviews and internal links should exist before content expansion.

A repair shop site should target phrases such as auto service website, brake repair, diagnostics and tire change paired with location intent. Service pages and case pages help Google understand topical relevance and commercial purpose.

After the service pages are published, add review markup, FAQ content, image optimization and a sitemap that exposes all important URLs. Then articles can support long-tail searches.
